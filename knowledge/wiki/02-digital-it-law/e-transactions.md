# Electronic Transactions (ธุรกรรมทางอิเล็กทรอนิกส์)

Electronic transactions refer to any civil, commercial, or governmental activity performed using electronic methods, as governed by the [[electronic-transactions-act-summary]].

## Key Definitions
- **Electronic Data:** Messages created, sent, received, or stored via electronic means (e.g., Email, EDI, Fax).
- **Electronic Transaction:** Transactions performed entirely or partially using electronic methods.

## Public Sector Integration (Section 35)
The Act allows government agencies to perform functions (e.g., applications, permits, registrations, payments) electronically. These actions have the same legal effect as traditional paper-based methods when performed according to the relevant Royal Decrees.

## Management Duties
- **Verification:** Data recipients should verify the sender's identity using agreed-upon methods.
- **Acknowledgment:** If a sender requires an acknowledgment of receipt, the data is not considered "sent" until the acknowledgment is received.
