# Computer Crime

Computer crime in Thailand is defined by the [[computer-crime-act-summary]]. It covers a wide range of activities that target or use computer systems and networks.

## Categories of Crime
1. **Targeting Systems/Data:** Unauthorized access, data theft, and hacking.
2. **Content-Related:** Fake news, defamation (via Section 16/1), and distribution of illegal material.
3. **Disruption:** Denial of Service (DoS) attacks and malware distribution.

## Key Definitions
- **Computer System:** A device or set of devices that work together to process data automatically.
- **Computer Data:** Messages, commands, or sets of commands in a form suitable for processing.

## Investigative Powers
Officials have the power to:
- Call persons to testify.
- Request [[traffic-data]] from service providers.
- With a court order: Copy data, access systems, and seize hardware.
