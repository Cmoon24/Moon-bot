# Debtor Rights

**Debtor Rights** refer to the legal protections afforded to individuals who owe money. These rights are designed to prevent abuse, harassment, and exploitation by creditors or debt collectors.

## Core Protections
- **Privacy**: The right to keep one's debt status private from third parties.
- **Fair Treatment**: Protection from physical or verbal abuse.
- **Accurate Information**: The right to receive clear information about the amount owed.
- **Recourse**: The right to file complaints with regulatory bodies if rights are violated.
