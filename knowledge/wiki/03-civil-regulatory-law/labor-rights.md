# Labor Rights

Labor rights in Thailand are primarily governed by the [[labor-protection-act-summary]], which guarantees minimum protections for all employees.

## Fundamental Rights
- **Right to Minimum Wage:** Entitlement to the daily minimum wage as determined by the Wage Committee.
- **Right to Leave:** Including sick leave, maternity leave, and personal business leave.
- **Right to a Safe Work Environment:** Protection from hazardous conditions and work-related injuries.
- **Right to Fair Termination:** Including notice periods and [[severance-pay]].

## Special Protections
- **Women:** Protected from hazardous work (e.g., underwater, in caves) and sexual harassment.
- **Children:** Hiring restricted to those 15 and older, with strict limits on hours and types of work for those under 18.
