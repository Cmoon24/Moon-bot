# Debt Collector Prohibitions

**Debt Collector Prohibitions** are specific actions that debt collection agencies and individuals are legally barred from performing.

## Prohibited Actions
- **Violence and Threats**: Using force or threatening physical harm.
- **Harassment**: Insulting language or excessive contact.
- **Disclosure**: Revealing debt information to employers, family (with exceptions), or the public.
- **Deception**: Posing as government officials or using fake legal documents.
- **Unfair Fees**: Charging unauthorized or excessive collection fees.
