# OCPB (Office of the Consumer Protection Board)

The **Office of the Consumer Protection Board (สคบ.)** is the primary government agency in Thailand responsible for protecting the rights of consumers.

## Role and Powers
- **Complaint Handling:** Receives and investigates complaints from consumers regarding unfair business practices.
- **Litigation:** Can initiate legal action against businesses on behalf of consumers. When the OCPB sues, they are exempt from all court fees.
- **Policy Making:** Recommends policies and measures for consumer protection to the Cabinet.
- **Monitoring:** Oversees advertising, labeling, and contract terms to ensure compliance with the law.

## Committees
The OCPB operates through several specialized committees:
- **Committee on Advertising:** Regulates misleading or false advertisements.
- **Committee on Labeling:** Ensures products have proper and accurate labels.
- **Committee on Safety:** Monitors and tracks products that may be hazardous.
- **Committee on Contracts:** Identifies "contract-controlled" businesses and ensures fair terms.
