# Land Title Deed

In Thailand, a **Land Title Deed** (โฉนดที่ดิน) is the primary document evidencing ownership of land. There are several types of documents representing different levels of rights.

## Types of Documents
- **Chanote (NS-4)**: The most secure form of land title, granting full ownership rights and accurately surveyed boundaries.
- **Nor Sor Sam (NS-3)**: A certificate of utilization. It proves that the person has possession of the land but boundaries may not be as precise as a Chanote.
- **Bai Chong**: A pre-emption certificate allowing temporary possession of land.

## Legal Requirements
- Transfers must be registered to be valid ([[land-code#Section 4 bis]]).
- Subject to revocation if abandoned ([[land-abandonment]]).
