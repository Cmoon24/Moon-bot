# Debt Collection Act B.E. 2558 (2015)

The **Debt Collection Act** is a regulatory framework in Thailand designed to balance the rights of creditors to recover debts and the rights of debtors to be free from harassment and unfair treatment.

## Major Provisions
- **Section 8**: Limits on contacting third parties.
- **Section 9**: Operational standards (time limits, identification).
- **Section 11**: Prohibition of violence, insults, and public disclosure.
- **Section 12**: Prohibition of false representation and deception.
- **Section 13**: Prohibition of unfair practices and excessive fees.
