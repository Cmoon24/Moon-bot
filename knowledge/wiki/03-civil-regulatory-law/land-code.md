# Land Code

The **Land Code** provides the legal framework for land administration, including land classification, ownership, possession, and the powers of the Department of Lands.

## Core Concepts
- [[land-title-deed]]: Legal documents proving ownership or possession.
- [[land-registration]]: The process of legally documenting land transactions.
- [[land-abandonment]]: The legal consequence of neglecting land for extended periods.

## Major Sections
- **Section 1-3**: Definitions and state ownership of land.
- **Section 4 bis**: Mandatory registration for valid transfers.
- **Section 6**: Revocation of rights due to abandonment.
- **Section 58 bis**: Issuance of title deeds through survey.
- **Section 61**: Power to revoke illegal documents.
