# Loan Contract

A **Loan Contract** is an agreement where one person (the lender) delivers a sum of money or other fungible things to another person (the borrower), who agrees to return things of the same kind, quality, and quantity.

## Key Principles (Thai Law)
- **Delivery**: The contract is complete only when the money is delivered (Section 650).
- **Evidence**: Written evidence is required for loans over 2,000 THB to be enforceable in court (Section 653).
- **Interest**: Capped at 15% per year by law ([[interest-rate-limit]]).
