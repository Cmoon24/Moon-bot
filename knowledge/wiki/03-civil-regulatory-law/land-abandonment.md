# Land Abandonment

**Land Abandonment** occurs when a landowner fails to utilize or maintain their land for a period specified by law. In Thailand, this can lead to the loss of land rights to the state.

## Legal Timeframes
- **Title Deeds (Chanote)**: 10 years of continuous neglect.
- **Certificates of Utilization (NS-3)**: 5 years of continuous neglect.

## Legal Process
Under Section 6 of the [[land-code]], the Director-General of the Department of Lands may file a petition with the court to revoke the land rights and return the land to the state.
