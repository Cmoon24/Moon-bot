# Guarantee Contract

A **Guarantee Contract** is a contract whereby a third person, called the **guarantor**, binds himself to the creditor to satisfy an obligation in the event the debtor fails to perform.

## Key Principles (Thai Law)
- **Accessory Contract**: Only valid for a valid primary debt (Section 681).
- **Written Evidence**: Required for enforcement (Section 680).
- **Notification**: Creditor must notify the guarantor within 60 days of debtor default (Section 686).
