# Slip Box

The **Slip Box** (German: *Zettelkasten*) is the central repository in the [[zettelkasten-method]]. It houses all notes and their connections.

> [!info] Semantic Origin
> The term *Zettel* refers to a "slip of paper," emphasizing the atomic nature of the system's components.

## Functionality
- Acts as an ==external brain== or "conversation partner."
- Facilitates the discovery of new connections during the process of filing and linking.
- Supports non-linear thinking and long-term knowledge accumulation.
