# Zettelkasten Method

The ==Zettelkasten Method== is a knowledge management framework based on the principle of linking **atomic notes**. Unlike traditional hierarchical filing, it allows for a web-like structure of information.

## Core Components
- [[slip-box]]: The physical or digital container for notes.
- **Atomic Notes**: Individual units of information.
- **IDs/Addresses**: Unique identifiers that allow for branching and linking.
