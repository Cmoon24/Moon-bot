# Niklas Luhmann

Niklas Luhmann (1927–1998) was a German sociologist and social theorist. He is widely recognized for his development of systems theory and his highly productive use of the [[zettelkasten-method]].

Luhmann credited his prolific output—including over 70 books—to his [[slip-box]] system, which he described as his "conversation partner."
