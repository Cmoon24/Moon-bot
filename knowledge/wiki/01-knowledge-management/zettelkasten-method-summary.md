# Zettelkasten Method (Summary)

The [[zettelkasten-method]] is a decentralized system for knowledge management popularized by the sociologist [[niklas-luhmann]]. It relies on the creation of a [[slip-box]] filled with interconnected, ==atomic notes==.

> [!tip] The Core Insight
> The value of a Zettelkasten is not in the storage of notes, but in the **connectivity** between them. It is a "conversation partner" that surprises you with new insights.

## Key Principles
- **Atomicity**: One idea per note.
- **Autonomy**: Notes are self-contained.
- **Connectivity**: Notes must be linked to form a web.
- **Organic Growth**: No predefined hierarchy; structure emerges bottom-up.
